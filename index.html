Who am I?

I am a 20 year-old data scientist and software engineer currently studying at Cal Poly, San Luis Obispo. I am in my third-year as a Computer Science major with a minor in Biology. I also work on-campus as a data science research assistant, teaching assistant for Systems Programming, and tutor with the Computer Science and Software Engineering Department. In my free time, I am an active member of the Cal Poly Triathlon Team.


Research

My current research interests are in the fields of data science and bioinformatics. I have been a research assistant at Cal Poly San Luis Obispo since September 2017, in the lab of Dr. Sean Lema. My primary work has consisted of performing PCA analyses and forming regression models in R to measure divergences in the morphology of marine populations. As some of our work is yet to be published, please contact me directly for any specific questions.
